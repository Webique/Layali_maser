WHO WE ARE

Welcome to Layali Maser, where the rich culture and flavors of Egypt come alive in the heart of Jeddah. Our café and lounge is a celebration of Egyptian hospitality, offering a warm atmosphere with authentic cuisine and beverages.

The Concept

At Layali Maser, we bring a slice of Egyptian nightlife and culinary delights to Jeddah. Whether you're here to relax with friends or enjoy a late-night experience, our lounge caters to your every need with vibrant ambiance and exceptional service.
